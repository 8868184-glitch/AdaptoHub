üî¨ Mendelian Inheritance

Gregor Mendel discovered the basic principles of heredity through pea plant experiments.

Key Concepts

Dominant vs. Recessive:
‚Ä¢ Dominant alleles (uppercase): Show their trait even with one copy
‚Ä¢ Recessive alleles (lowercase): Need two copies to show trait

T = tall (dominant), t = short (recessive)
‚Ä¢ TT or Tt = Tall plant
‚Ä¢ tt = Short plant

‚Ä¢ 3 Tall : 1 Short (phenotypes)
