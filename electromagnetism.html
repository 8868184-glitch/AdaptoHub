üì° Electromagnetic Waves

When electric and magnetic fields oscillate together, they create electromagnetic waves that propagate through space at the speed of light.

Maxwell's Equations

James Clerk Maxwell unified electricity and magnetism into four fundamental equations that describe all electromagnetic phenomena. These equations predicted the existence of electromagnetic waves.
